FN SCAR®-L MK2

For the most demanding missions.

The FN SCAR®-L Mk2 assault rifle is chambered in 5.56x45mm NATO calibre and comes standard with a choice of barrel lengths (10” or 14.5”) to fit any requirements in terms of user morphology and mission at hand. These barrels are interchangeable in less than five minutes.
