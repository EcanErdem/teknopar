FN HERSTAL

A UNIQUE PORTFOLIO FOR DEFENCE & SECURITY

We design, develop, manufacture and sell a complete range of state-of-the-art, ground-breaking solutions developed around small calibre firearms and associated ammunition. These are deployed by military, security and special forces worldwide.

A GLOBAL COMPANY

Since its inception in 1889, the company has been based in Herstal, close to Liege in Belgium, at the heart of Europe, the European Union and NATO. While the Belgium-based facilities house all departments in charge of designing, developing, manufacturing, testing, marketing and selling FN solutions, FN Herstal is the parent company of three fully owned subsidiaries:
- FN America (States of America)
- FNH UK (United Kingdom)
- Noptel (Finland)
